Common Myths About SEO We Hear Every Day

Over the years, we’ve had hundreds of conversations with business owners, and we often hear the same concerns. Here are a few myths we’d like to clear up:

  • “SEO is a one-time project.”
    SEO isn’t something you set and forget. It requires continuous work, especially as competitors improve and search engines update their algorithms.
  • “More traffic means more sales.”
    While traffic is important, we focus on the right traffic. We want visitors who are ready to engage, call, or buy—not just browse.
  • “Results should happen overnight.”
    SEO is a long game. We see positive movement within months, but the most stable growth happens gradually. Patience pays off.
